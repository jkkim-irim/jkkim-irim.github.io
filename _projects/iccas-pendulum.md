🥉 **Bronze (3rd place) — 2nd Koh Young AI Competition, ICCAS 2024.**

At the **2nd Koh Young AI Competition**, held at the 24th International Conference on
Control, Automation and Systems (**ICCAS 2024**, Jeju, Korea), our team **Pluto X**
(KOREATECH) tackled the **swing-up and stabilization of a 3-degree-of-freedom (3-DOF)
inverted pendulum**, earning the Bronze award (3rd place).
